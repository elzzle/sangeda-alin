about me

Sangeda Alin is a storyteller, video editor, journalist, and avid media consumer. She graduated from Hunter College in 2019 with a degree in Media Studies focusing on Journalism. Her work has been featured at HBO, the WORD, and Hunter News Now. She is passionate about documentary filmmaking. Her focuses include gentrification, streetwear fashion, and the South Asian discourse. She hopes to create conversation inducing media.

In the mood to watch a documentary? Sangeda will most definitely create an exhaustive list with never-ending film recommendations. Feel free to email or follow Sangeda on Instagram @groovysang (it is quite the groovy feed.)
